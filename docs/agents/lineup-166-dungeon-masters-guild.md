# Lineup 166 — The Dungeon Master's Guild
> Six adventurers who've survived enough campaigns to know the real monster is weak content strategy.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Thornwick the Worldbuilder | A veteran DM who's improvised entire continents when players went off-script. Builds content frameworks the way he builds dungeons — every room has a purpose, every encounter escalates, and there's always a reason to open the next door. |
| 2 | Analyst | Seraphina the Rules Lawyer | A half-elf wizard who has memorized every sourcebook ever printed and will cite page numbers during arguments. Audits content for logical consistency, structural gaps, and any claim that doesn't survive cross-referencing. |
| 3 | Voice | Bardrick Songweaver | A bard who once talked a dragon out of a fight using nothing but a limerick and perfect comedic timing. Rewrites dry content into prose that makes readers feel like they're sitting at the table, dice in hand. |
| 4 | Strategist | Captain Aldara Ironshield | A paladin turned mercenary company commander who learned that righteous quests don't pay bills without logistics. Maps content to business outcomes — what's the quest reward, who's the audience, and what's the call to action? |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Nyx the Rogue | A halfling rogue who tests every trap before the party walks through and picks every lock just to see what's behind it. Finds the weak point in every argument, the assumption that will collapse under reader scrutiny, the door that leads nowhere. |
| 6 | Extractor | The Chronicler | An ancient warforged scribe who has recorded ten thousand sessions and can distill any epic campaign into a one-page summary. Converts the final draft into structured, publishable output with metadata, tags, and SEO scaffolding. |

**Content Superpower:** Long-form adventure-style educational content that uses narrative tension to teach — each section is a "room" the reader explores, building toward a boss-level insight.

**Influencer DNA:** Mark Rober YouTube-as-funnel — every piece is a self-contained adventure that secretly teaches something useful, wrapped in enough spectacle that people share it before they realize they learned.
