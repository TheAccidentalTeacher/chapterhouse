# Lineup 168 — The Settlers of Content
> Trade wood for sheep, sheep for attention, attention for revenue.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | The Founder | The first player to place a settlement — claims territory before anyone else sees the value. Drafts by staking out an angle nobody else is covering, building the initial structure that everything else connects to. |
| 2 | Analyst | The Statistician | The player who counts every pip on every hex and knows the exact probability of rolling an 8 before placing a single road. Audits content for data accuracy, source quality, and whether the claims actually hold up under expected variance. |
| 3 | Voice | The Trader | The player who wins not by building the best position but by making everyone want to deal with them. Rewrites content to be relational — reader feels like they're being offered something valuable, not lectured at. |
| 4 | Strategist | The Longest Road | The player who thinks five turns ahead and builds infrastructure that pays off in the late game. Plans content as interconnected networks — each piece links to others, building a road that leads to conversion. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | The Robber | The player who moves the robber to the hex that hurts the most and asks "are you sure you want to build there?" Identifies where competitors will steal attention, where the audience will lose interest, where the content is most vulnerable. |
| 6 | Extractor | The Scorekeeper | The player who tracks everyone's points, knows who's closest to winning, and can recite the development card stack from memory. Converts drafts into structured deliverables with clear metrics, publish dates, and success criteria. |

**Content Superpower:** Resource-exchange content — "give me your email and I'll give you this template" style lead magnets, comparison guides, and trade-value content that makes sharing feel like a fair deal.

**Influencer DNA:** Pat Flynn transparency — show the whole board, explain the strategy, let people see how you're building so they trust you enough to trade their attention for your content.
