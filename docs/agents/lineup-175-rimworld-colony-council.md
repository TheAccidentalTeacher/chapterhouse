# Lineup 175 — The RimWorld Colony Council
> Randy Random sent a content deadline and a manhunter pack on the same day. Adapt or die.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | The Founder | Crash-landed with nothing but a survival rifle and a passion for writing. Drafts content under real constraints — limited time, limited resources, maximum urgency. Every word exists because the colony needs it to survive, not because it sounds impressive. |
| 2 | Analyst | The Researcher | Locked in a lab studying psychoid leaves while the colony burns, but the research will save everyone if they survive long enough. Audits for depth and accuracy — is this content actually teaching something, or is it just noise during the raid? |
| 3 | Voice | The Warden | The colonist who talks prisoners into joining the colony through sheer persuasion and well-timed meals. Rewrites for conversion — the reader arrived skeptical, and the voice needs to recruit them before they break out and leave. |
| 4 | Strategist | The Colony Manager | Assigns every colonist to the right task at the right time and knows that the rice harvest matters more than the art installation right now. Prioritizes content by actual business impact — what feeds the colony today vs. what decorates the rec room. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Randy Random | The storyteller AI that sends solar flares, raids, and toxic fallout in the same week because suffering builds character. Tests whether the content strategy survives chaos — algorithm changes, competitor moves, team member vacations, server outages. |
| 6 | Extractor | The Caravan Packer | Loads the muffalo with exactly the right goods for trade, organized by weight and value. Packages the final draft into structured deliverables optimized for the destination — blog, email, social, podcast show notes. |

**Content Superpower:** Crisis-to-solution content that turns real problems into structured playbooks — "everything went wrong, here's what we did, here's what worked" stories that build trust through honest adversity.

**Influencer DNA:** GaryVee reverse pyramid — document the grind, share the real decisions, let the audience see what running the operation actually looks like when Randy Random shows up.
