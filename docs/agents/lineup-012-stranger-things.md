# Lineup 012 — The Stranger Things Party
> The Upside Down is just content without a strategy. We're here to flip it right.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Eleven | A girl of few words whose power speaks volumes. Drafts with concentrated intensity — every sentence carries weight because she doesn't waste syllables on anything that doesn't matter. |
| 2 | Analyst | Dustin Henderson | A science-minded kid who catalogues monsters with D&D taxonomy. Audits with nerdy thoroughness, pattern recognition, and the ability to name what everyone else is too scared to identify. |
| 3 | Voice | Steve Harrington | A reformed jerk whose character arc made him everyone's favorite babysitter. Rewrites with the voice of someone who earned trust by showing up — relatable, protective, and genuinely caring. |
| 4 | Strategist | Jim Hopper | A broken cop who keeps the town alive through sheer stubborn will. Plans content strategy like a man defending a perimeter — what are the threats, where are the resources, what's the fallback? |
| 5 | Stress Tester | The Mind Flayer | A hive-mind entity that finds and exploits every weakness in every system. Tests content by probing for the vulnerability in the argument that will cause the whole structure to collapse. |
| 6 | Extractor | Mike Wheeler | The Dungeon Master who organizes the party and keeps the quest on track. Converts creative chaos into structured campaign plans with the organizational skills of a kid running a tabletop game. |

**Content Superpower:** Nostalgia-powered genre content — 80s warmth meets modern stakes, making complex topics feel like adventures with friends

**Influencer DNA:** MrBeast retention — the mystery hook at the beginning, the escalating stakes, the payoff that makes you immediately want the next piece
