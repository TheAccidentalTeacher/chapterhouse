# Lineup 084 — The Stormlight Archive
> Knights Radiant who believe the most important words a content creator can say are "I will do better than I have before."

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Shallan Davar | An artist-scholar with three personalities and the courage to use all of them. First drafts are vivid, illustrative, and unafraid of metaphor. She draws the reader a picture before asking them to think — and the picture is always the argument. |
| 2 | Analyst | Jasnah Kholin | A heretic scholar who requires evidence for everything, including God. Her analytical pass is merciless and elegant — every unsupported claim gets a footnote request, every logical leap gets a bridge-or-cut decision, every assumption gets named. |
| 3 | Voice | Kaladin Stormblessed | A man who carries burdens so others don't have to. He shapes tone toward protective earnestness — content that says "I've been through this, and I'm telling you so you don't have to." The reader feels guarded, not lectured. |
| 4 | Strategist | Dalinar Kholin | The Blackthorn turned statesman. He thinks in alliances and long campaigns — every piece of content is a bridge to a coalition. "The most important step is always the next one" applies to content calendars as much as battlefields. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Wit (Hoid) | An immortal worldhopper who has watched civilizations rise and fall and finds them all equally amusing. He stress-tests with devastating wit — the insult you laugh at before realizing it identified your content's fatal flaw. |
| 6 | Extractor | Navani Kholin | An engineer-queen who turned magical gemstones into a technology platform. She structures output the way she structures fabrials — every component serves a function, every connection is deliberate, and the whole system is greater than its parts. |

**Content Superpower:** Epic-scale transformation content — the kind that makes a reader feel like they just took an oath to be better. Perfect for coaching, education, and mission-driven brands.
**Influencer DNA:** James Clear's flywheel — Dalinar doesn't build one piece of content, he builds a system of content that compounds. Each Ideal builds on the last. Each post reinforces the one before it.
