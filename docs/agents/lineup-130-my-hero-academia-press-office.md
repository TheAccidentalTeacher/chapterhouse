# Lineup 130 — My Hero Academia Press Office
> Plus Ultra content — for audiences learning to use powers they didn't know they had.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Izuku Midoriya (Deku) | The kid who was born without a quirk and became the greatest hero. Deku drafts with the obsessive note-taking energy of someone who studied every hero in the book because he couldn't rely on natural talent. His content is over-researched, deeply informed, and written for underdogs. |
| 2 | Analyst | Shota Aizawa (Eraserhead) | He can erase your quirk with a look. Aizawa audits by stripping the content of every gimmick, every clever trick, every stylistic flourish — and seeing if anything worthwhile remains. If the content works without its quirk, it's ready. If not, back to the drawing board. |
| 3 | Voice | Ochaco Uraraka | Zero gravity hero who fights to support her family. Ochaco's voice is hopeful, grounded, and financially aware. She writes for readers who are building something real with limited resources — her content never forgets that the audience has a budget and a mortgage. |
| 4 | Strategist | All Might | Symbol of Peace, master of brand positioning. All Might's strategy is aspirational — he understands that sometimes the content isn't about information, it's about making the audience believe they can do it. His distribution plan puts the right message in front of the person who needs hope. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Katsuki Bakugo | "DIE!" — Bakugo stress-tests with explosive aggression. He doesn't find polite weaknesses; he finds the thing that makes him want to destroy the content and replace it with something better. His feedback is violent, personal, and almost always correct. |
| 6 | Extractor | Tenya Iida | Class president, engine-legged speedster, and the most organized student at UA. Iida extracts with robotic efficiency and genuine care — every output is formatted to spec, every deadline is met early, every deliverable includes a cover note explaining what's inside. He runs to deliver it. |

**Content Superpower:** Underdog empowerment content — beginner guides, "start from zero" series, and content that makes first-time entrepreneurs feel like they belong in the room.
**Influencer DNA:** Dad voice homeschool meets Hormozi give-away-secrets — written by someone who started with nothing, for someone who's starting with nothing, with the full playbook included.
