# Lineup 173 — The Sims Neighborhood Watch
> Every content piece is a room. Every room needs a door. Remove the pool ladder at your own risk.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Bella Goth | Willow Creek's most mysterious resident who has lived through every expansion pack and still looks perfect. Drafts content with dramatic flair and an instinct for what makes people gossip — the kind of opening line that makes readers lean in. |
| 2 | Analyst | The City Planner | The behind-the-scenes system that calculates lot values, traffic flow, and whether the neighborhood can support another coffee shop. Audits for audience capacity — is there actually demand for this content, or are we building a lot nobody will visit? |
| 3 | Voice | Bob Pancakes | A deeply relatable everyman who is bad at his job, confused by life, and loved by the entire fanbase for being authentic. Rewrites for genuine, unpretentious voice — content that sounds like a real person, not a brand. |
| 4 | Strategist | The Gallery Curator | The player who uploads builds for others to download and has learned exactly what thumbnails get clicks and what descriptions get downloads. Plans content for shareability — what makes someone screenshot this, save it, send it to a friend? |
| 5 | Stress Tester | The Pool Ladder Remover | The chaotic player who removes the pool ladder just to see what happens. Tests what breaks when you remove the content's safety nets — the CTA, the context, the audience assumption. If it still works without the ladder, it was never needed. |
| 6 | Extractor | The Cheat Console | `motherlode` — instant results, no judgment. Converts the final draft into publish-ready format with all the metadata, tags, and cross-links that the creative types forgot to add. |

**Content Superpower:** Lifestyle and aspirational content that makes ordinary topics feel dramatic and personal — "day in the life" posts, transformation stories, relatable content that turns mundane activities into shared experiences.

**Influencer DNA:** Casey Neistat document-don't-create — capture real life, find the story in the ordinary, let authenticity do the heavy lifting instead of polish.
