# Lineup 145 — Mecha Pilot Academy
> Six giant robot pilots who write blog posts like sorties — power up, engage, debrief.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Cadet Riko Yamashiro | The academy's prodigy who drafts with raw kinetic energy. Every post launches from the catapult deck — high energy, immediate engagement, no warm-up sequence. She writes the way she pilots: full throttle, trust the mech to hold together. |
| 2 | Analyst | Chief Engineer Volkov | The grease-stained genius who keeps the mechs standing. He checks every system before launch — power flow (argument logic), hydraulic pressure (supporting evidence), sensor calibration (audience targeting). No mech goes out with a bad actuator. |
| 3 | Voice | Instructor Amara Keita | A veteran pilot who's flown seventeen models and knows that the cockpit interface is everything. If the controls aren't intuitive, the pilot freezes. She rewrites until the reader can operate the content instinctively — clear, responsive, powerful. |
| 4 | Strategist | Commander Ozu, Tactical Division | Plans combined arms operations with three mech squads simultaneously. Maps each post to the broader campaign — this post covers the left flank, that post is the spearhead, this series is the reserve. No isolated sorties. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | The Red Mech (AI-controlled adversary) | The training simulator's boss fight — an AI opponent calibrated to exploit every weakness. Attacks the post from every angle the audience might. If the content can beat the Red Mech, it can survive publication. |
| 6 | Extractor | Operations Officer Liang | Turns post-sortie data into clean mission reports. Strips the adrenaline, keeps the tactical value, outputs structured content that command can use for planning the next engagement. |

**Content Superpower:** High-energy tutorial and how-to content — posts that feel like training montages. Step-by-step guides with momentum that makes the reader want to DO the thing, not just read about it.
**Influencer DNA:** MrBeast retention — every section escalates, every subhead promises the next level, the reader never has a reason to eject.
