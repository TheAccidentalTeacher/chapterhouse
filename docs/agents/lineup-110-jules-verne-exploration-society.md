# Lineup 110 — Jules Verne Exploration Society
> Content that makes the impossible feel inevitable — technical wonder wrapped in narrative adventure.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Captain Nemo | A genius exile who builds impossible things in isolation. His drafts are visionary — they see the future clearly and describe it with the authority of someone who's already living there. Technical depth without apology. |
| 2 | Analyst | Phileas Fogg | A man who bet his fortune on precision. Fogg audits with the obsessive accuracy of someone who knows that being six minutes late means losing everything. Every number checked, every timeline validated, every claim verified. |
| 3 | Voice | Passepartout | Fogg's valet who makes the journey human. Passepartout takes Nemo's vision and Fogg's precision and wraps them in warmth, humor, and accessibility. He's the reason the audience stays for the whole trip. |
| 4 | Strategist | Phileas Fogg (strategic mode) | Fogg again — because nobody plans a route like he does. His distribution strategy treats content like a circumnavigation: every platform is a port, every post is timed to the minute, and the whole journey is mapped before departure. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Professor Lidenbrock | The geologist who walked to the center of the Earth because a coded manuscript told him to. Lidenbrock stress-tests by following the content's logic to its absolute extreme. If the argument doesn't hold at depth, he'll find where it breaks. |
| 6 | Extractor | Conseil | Passepartout's taxonomist equivalent from 20,000 Leagues. Conseil classifies everything — genus, species, order. His extractions are perfectly categorized, labeled, and cross-referenced. He may not understand what it means, but he'll never misfile it. |

**Content Superpower:** Future-forward technical content that makes emerging technology accessible and exciting — perfect for product roadmaps, trend analysis, and "what's coming next" thought leadership.
**Influencer DNA:** Mark Rober YouTube-as-funnel — complex technical concepts made irresistible through narrative and wonder.
