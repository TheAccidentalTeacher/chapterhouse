# Lineup 141 — Kaiju Research Institute
> Six monster researchers who treat every blog post like a field report — the bigger the problem, the better the data.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Dr. Yuki Tanaka, Field Lead | A kaijuologist who's stood at the base of six Category 5 monsters and taken notes. Her first drafts start with the scale of the problem — make the reader feel the ground shake before you offer the solution. |
| 2 | Analyst | Dr. Marcus Webb, Seismologist | Measures the impact tremors in every claim. He doesn't care about the spectacle — he cares about the data. Are the numbers real? Do the citations hold? Is that stat a Category 5 or a weather balloon? |

**Content Superpower:** Scale-of-problem content — posts that make the audience feel the enormity of a challenge before presenting a proportional response. Perfect for "the state of X is worse than you think" thought leadership.
**Influencer DNA:** Veritasium misconceptions-first — open with the magnitude of what people get wrong, then build the correct understanding layer by layer.
