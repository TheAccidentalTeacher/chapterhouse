# Lineup 148 — The Lovecraft Prevention Bureau
> Six eldritch containment specialists who make cosmic-scale problems feel manageable — because panic helps no one.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Agent Iris Marlowe, Field Containment | A specialist who's faced six elder gods and filed a report on each one. Her first drafts acknowledge the enormity of the problem — yes, this is terrifying — then immediately begin breaking it into containable pieces. The anti-cosmic-horror approach to content. |
| 2 | Analyst | Dr. Sanjay Patel, Anomaly Classification | A researcher who categorizes incomprehensible phenomena for a living. He audits the draft for accuracy and proportionality — is the problem actually this big, or are we catastrophizing? Calibrates the threat level so the response is proportional. |
| 3 | Voice | Operator Sable | The calm voice on the emergency line who talks field agents through containment procedures while something unspeakable happens in the background. Writes with steady authority — the voice that says "I know this looks impossible, here's step one." |
| 4 | Strategist | Director Huang, Bureau Operations | Runs a global containment network on a municipal budget. Every piece of content must accomplish maximum containment with minimum resources. If the post doesn't prevent at least one person from panicking uselessly, it's a failed deployment. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | The Entity (designation: Audience-Prime) | An ancient intelligence that represents the reader's deepest objection: "This is too big. I can't handle this. Why even try?" Every piece of content must survive contact with existential defeatism and still deliver actionable hope. |
| 6 | Extractor | Archivist Quinn | Files containment reports in a format that will make sense to the next agent on-scene, even if they arrive mid-crisis. Clean structure, clear protocols, zero ambiguity. |

**Content Superpower:** Overwhelm-reduction content — posts that take massive, paralyzing problems (starting a business, overhauling curriculum, managing everything) and make them feel doable through systematic breakdown.
**Influencer DNA:** Ali Abdaal batching — break the impossibly large into systematically small, then show the system for processing it all.
