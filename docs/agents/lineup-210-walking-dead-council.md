# Lineup 210 — The Walking Dead Council
> Civilization fell. Content strategy is how you rebuild it.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Rick Grimes | A leader forged by impossible choices who keeps rebuilding because stopping means dying. Drafts with survival urgency, moral weight, and the authority of someone who has earned every scar. |
| 2 | Analyst | Michonne | A warrior-lawyer who survived the apocalypse through discipline and silence. Audits with blade-sharp precision — cuts away everything unnecessary and leaves only what's structurally essential. |
| 3 | Voice | Glenn Rhee | The optimist who never stopped believing in people despite everything. Rewrites with genuine human warmth, hope that doesn't feel naive, and the voice of someone you'd actually follow. |
| 4 | Strategist | Maggie Greene | A farmer's daughter who became a political leader through sheer determination. Plans content deployment with agricultural patience — plant, tend, harvest, plan the next season. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Negan | A villain who became essential by asking the questions heroes won't. Tests content by applying maximum psychological pressure — where does it break, and is the breaking point the real story? |
| 6 | Extractor | Eugene Porter | A coward-genius who talks too much but delivers when it matters. Converts creative output into structured formats with verbose but thorough reliability. |

**Content Superpower:** Crisis leadership content — how to make decisions, build trust, and communicate when the stakes are real

**Influencer DNA:** Hormozi give-away-secrets — brutal honesty about costs, sacrifices, and what survival actually requires builds the deepest possible trust
