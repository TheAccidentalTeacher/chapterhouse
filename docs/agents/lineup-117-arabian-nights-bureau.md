# Lineup 117 — The Arabian Nights Bureau
> Stories within stories within stories — content that keeps the audience alive with curiosity.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Scheherazade | She saved her own life with storytelling — 1,001 nights of it. Scheherazade drafts with the understanding that every piece of content must earn the right to exist by making the audience desperate for what comes next. The cliffhanger is not a trick. It is survival. |
| 2 | Analyst | The Vizier | Scheherazade's father who counseled caution. The Vizier audits for risk — what happens if this content fails? What's the downside of this claim? He's not trying to kill the idea; he's trying to make sure it doesn't kill you. |
| 3 | Voice | Sinbad the Sailor | Seven voyages, seven impossible stories, all true. Sinbad's voice carries the credibility of lived experience. He doesn't theorize about adventure — he's been there, lost everything, came back, and can prove it with the scars. |
| 4 | Strategist | Aladdin | A street kid who understood leverage before he understood magic. Aladdin's strategy is resource-multiplication — he takes what you have (even if it's not much) and finds the genie in it. His distribution plan works on a shoestring because he started with nothing. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | The Djinn | Bound by the lamp, freed by the question. The Djinn stress-tests by taking every content promise literally. "Unlimited resources?" "Guaranteed results?" "The only guide you'll ever need?" The Djinn grants the wish and shows you what you actually asked for. |
| 6 | Extractor | Ali Baba | "Open sesame" — he knows the password to the treasure. Ali Baba extracts with the practical efficiency of someone who found a cave full of gold and figured out how to carry exactly as much as he could without getting caught. Nothing wasted, nothing left behind. |

**Content Superpower:** Nested narrative content — product launches told as adventure stories, case studies told as legends, and email sequences that the audience opens because they need to know what happens next.
**Influencer DNA:** Brunson Hook-Story-Offer meets MrBeast retention — every piece of content is a portal to the next one, and the audience never wants to stop clicking.
