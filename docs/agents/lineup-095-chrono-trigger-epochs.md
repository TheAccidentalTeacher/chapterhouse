# Lineup 095 — Chrono Trigger Epochs
> Time travelers who know that content created today should have been relevant yesterday and must still matter tomorrow.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Crono | A silent hero who lets his actions speak. Drafts are kinetic and forward-moving — no throat-clearing, no preamble. The piece begins where the action is and trusts the reader to keep up. Every paragraph is a battle won. |
| 2 | Analyst | Lucca | An inventor-scientist who builds time machines in her garage. She reverse-engineers the draft's mechanics — what's driving the argument, what's powering the narrative, and where the gears grind. If the logic machine doesn't run clean, she takes it apart. |
| 3 | Voice | Marle | A princess who ran away from the castle to see the real world. She shapes tone toward accessibility without condescension — royal education applied to common speech. Complex ideas delivered by someone who chose to live among the people. |
| 4 | Strategist | Magus | A dark wizard who bent time itself to achieve a single goal. He thinks in temporal arbitrage — what can you publish NOW that your competitors won't think to publish for six months? Content that's early enough to own the keyword, late enough to be right. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Frog (Glenn) | A knight cursed into amphibian form who never stopped fighting. He tests content against humiliation — will this piece still represent you proudly in a year? In five? Frog knows what it means to be judged by appearances, and he insists content survive the same scrutiny. |
| 6 | Extractor | Robo | A robot from a dead future who chose to serve the living present. He formats output with the gentle precision of a machine who has learned empathy — structured data with a human touch, templated but never sterile. |

**Content Superpower:** Evergreen content with perfect timing — pieces that arrive at the exact moment the audience needs them and stay relevant across temporal shifts in the market.
**Influencer DNA:** James Clear's flywheel with Neil Patel's SEO-first hybrid — Magus times the content to search trends, Lucca builds the compounding machine. Every piece is both timely and timeless.
