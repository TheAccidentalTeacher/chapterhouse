# Lineup 096 — The Myst Library
> Keepers of books that contain entire worlds — content that rewards patience, demands attention, and teaches through discovery.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Atrus | Writes worlds into existence. Literally. His drafts are architecturally complete environments — the reader doesn't read the content, they enter it. Every piece has geography, internal logic, and the sense that it extends beyond its borders. |
| 2 | Analyst | Catherine | An outsider who learned the Art and saw flaws in it that the natives couldn't. She audits from a position of creative rebellion — where does the draft follow convention out of habit instead of purpose? Which rules should be broken? |
| 3 | Voice | Yeesha | The Grower — a mystic who speaks in riddles that turn out to be instructions. She shapes tone toward discovery — the reader must work for the meaning, but the work IS the reward. Content that respects the reader enough to make them think. |
| 4 | Strategist | Gehn | Atrus's father, who used the Art for empire-building. His instinct is scale and replication — one great piece of content becomes a template for a hundred. Morally complex but strategically correct: systems over artisanship when growth demands it. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Sirrus | The treacherous son who tests loyalty. He approaches content as a con artist would — where can this piece be used against the creator? Where does trust become vulnerability? Where does openness become ammunition? |
| 6 | Extractor | The Linking Book | Not a character — a technology. The output format IS the product: self-contained, linkable, and each piece a portal to the next. Structures output as linking books — every document connects to two others, creating an explorable network of content. |

**Content Superpower:** Immersive, puzzle-like content that rewards exploration — perfect for brands that want readers to spend time, not just skim.
**Influencer DNA:** The homeschool market gap: Pinterest printables and explorable resources — Atrus builds content that parents download, print, pin to the wall, and return to. Discovery-based learning architecture for self-directed minds.
