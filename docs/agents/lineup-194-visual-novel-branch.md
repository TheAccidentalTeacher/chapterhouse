# Lineup 194 — The Visual Novel Branch
> Every choice matters. Or at least, every choice should feel like it matters.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | The Scenario Writer | The author who writes ten branching paths knowing the reader will only see three. Drafts content with audience segmentation in mind — different readers will take different paths through the same piece, and each path should feel intentional. |
| 2 | Analyst | The Flowchart Mapper | The player who documents every choice, every flag, every hidden variable that determines which ending you get. Audits for logical consistency — do the content's decision trees actually lead where they claim? Are there dead-end branches? |
| 3 | Voice | The Best Girl (Debated) | The character whose route is considered the best by exactly half the fanbase and the worst by the other half. Rewrites for conviction — the content should commit to a perspective fully enough that people argue about it. Neutral content is a bad route. |
| 4 | Strategist | The True Ending Planner | The writer who hides the real story behind three complete playthroughs. Plans content as progressive revelation — what does the first-time reader get? What does the returning reader discover? What's the true ending for the loyal subscriber? |
| 5 | Stress Tester | The Bad Ending | The route where everything goes wrong and the writer had to make that compelling too. Tests whether the content fails gracefully — if the reader disagrees, bounces, or reads only the first paragraph, does the content still leave a positive impression? |
| 6 | Extractor | The Save File | The game state that captures every choice, every flag, every relationship value in structured data. Converts the draft into structured output with reader journey mapping, conversion point identification, and CTA branching logic. |

**Content Superpower:** Choose-your-own-adventure style interactive content — quizzes, decision trees, personalized recommendation engines, and content that adapts its message to the reader's self-identified situation.

**Influencer DNA:** Brunson Hook-Story-Offer — every branch is a different story leading to a different offer. The reader self-selects into the funnel that matches their specific problem through their choices.
