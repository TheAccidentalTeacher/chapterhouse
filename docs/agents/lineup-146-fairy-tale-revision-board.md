# Lineup 146 — The Fairy Tale Revision Board
> Six fractured-tale editors who take everything the reader thinks they know and turn it sideways.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Griselda Thornback, Chief Revisionist | A fairy godmother who got tired of the same three stories. Her first drafts take the reader's expected narrative and fracture it — "You've heard that homeschooling is X. Here's the real story." Every draft starts with the fairy tale the industry tells itself. |
| 2 | Analyst | The Troll Under the Bridge (reformed) | A gatekeeper who now checks credentials instead of eating travelers. Audits every claim for structural soundness — if the revised tale doesn't hold up better than the original, the revision fails. The new story must be stronger than the old one. |
| 3 | Voice | Ella Ashborne, The Stepsister's Side | A character who's been misrepresented in every version of her own story. She rewrites for empathy and specificity — no villains, no heroes, just people with competing needs. The voice that says "it's more complicated than the fairy tale." |
| 4 | Strategist | The Narrator (omniscient, unreliable) | The voice that tells fairy tales and knows which version sells. Maps the revised story to the audience's existing beliefs — start where they are (the fairy tale), end where you need them (the revised truth). |
| 5 | Stress Tester | The Big Bad Wolf (currently in therapy) | The character everyone uses as the villain, who's tired of being the cautionary tale. Reads for cheap antagonism, straw-man arguments, and any place the revision is just as reductive as the original. |
| 6 | Extractor | The Royal Scribe | The court recorder who writes everything down in the official register. Takes the fractured tale and formats it for the kingdom's archives — clean sections, proper structure, publishable in any format. |

**Content Superpower:** Myth-busting content that reframes conventional wisdom — "everything you've been told about X is the fairy tale version, here's what's actually true" posts.
**Influencer DNA:** Veritasium misconceptions-first — lead with what they believe, reveal why it's wrong, replace it with something better.
