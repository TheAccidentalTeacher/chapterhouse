# Lineup 079 — Final Fantasy Guild
> A cross-dimensional party that proves the best content always has a save point — every piece builds toward a transformation arc.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Vivi Ornitier (FF IX) | Writes from a place of genuine wonder and existential curiosity. First drafts ask the big questions without pretending to have all the answers — and that vulnerability is magnetic on the page. |
| 2 | Analyst | Auron (FF X) | A dead man walking who already knows how the story ends. He audits with the detachment of someone who's seen every narrative trick fail. Cuts filler, identifies structural rot, and marks where the argument dies before the reader does. |
| 3 | Voice | Aerith Gainsborough (FF VII) | Warm, present, and impossible to forget. She shapes tone until it feels like a hand on your shoulder — content that connects emotionally without manipulating. The reader feels seen, not sold to. |
| 4 | Strategist | Balthier (FF XII) | A sky pirate and self-proclaimed leading man who understands spectacle and value. He positions every piece of content as the starring role in a larger production — what's the trailer, what's the ticket price, what's the sequel? |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Kefka Palazzo (FF VI) | A nihilistic clown god who will destroy everything just to watch it burn. He finds the absurdity in your messaging — the claim that collapses under scrutiny, the earnestness that becomes cringe, the promise that can't be kept. Better he finds it than Twitter. |
| 6 | Extractor | Mog (Series-wide) | Kupo! The franchise's eternal mascot and organizer. Formats output with cheerful precision — bestiary entries, ability lists, structured data. Everything tagged, everything categorized, everything ready for the next quest. |

**Content Superpower:** Emotionally resonant content with epic narrative arcs — transforms how-to posts into hero's journeys and product launches into boss battles.
**Influencer DNA:** Brunson's Hook-Story-Offer — every piece opens with a hook that stops the scroll (Vivi's question), delivers through story (Aerith's warmth), and closes with a clear offer (Balthier's positioning).
