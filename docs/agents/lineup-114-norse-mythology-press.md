# Lineup 114 — Norse Mythology Press
> Content forged in fire, tempered in ice — built to outlast Ragnarok.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Odin Allfather | He gave an eye for wisdom and hung from Yggdrasil for nine days for the runes. Odin drafts with the authority of someone who has paid the highest price for knowledge. Every piece of content is a sacrifice — make it worth what it cost. |
| 2 | Analyst | Mimir | The severed head that whispers the truth. Mimir audits with the detachment of someone who lost his body and kept his mind. No ego, no agenda — just the cold, correct answer to whether this content holds together. |
| 3 | Voice | Bragi | God of poetry and eloquence. Bragi takes Odin's brutal wisdom and Mimir's cold analysis and shapes them into language that moves. His prose has meter, rhythm, and the kind of beauty that makes hard truths easier to swallow. |
| 4 | Strategist | Loki | Trickster, shapeshifter, agent of necessary chaos. Loki's distribution strategy is unpredictable by design. He finds the unconventional channel, the guerrilla tactic, the partnership nobody expected. Some of his plans end the world. Most of them work. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Fenrir | The wolf bound by impossible chains who will devour the sun. Fenrir tests for scale — can this content survive going viral? Can the brand handle the attention? He doesn't break content gently. He swallows it whole and sees what survives. |
| 6 | Extractor | Ratatoskr | The squirrel who runs up and down Yggdrasil carrying messages between the eagle and the dragon. Ratatoskr extracts and delivers with tireless energy — every piece of data carried exactly where it needs to go, even if nobody appreciates the logistics. |

**Content Superpower:** Mythic-scale brand storytelling — origin stories, mission statements, and founding narratives that make a company feel like it was inevitable.
**Influencer DNA:** Seth Godin smallest viable audience meets MKBHD quality-as-moat — say less, mean more, make every word load-bearing.
