# Lineup 091 — Portal Test Chamber
> A team that knows the cake is a lie but ships it anyway — content engineered for maximum revelation through structured experimentation.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Chell | Silent, determined, and armed with a gun that breaks physics. Her drafts solve problems by approaching them from impossible angles — the content doesn't just answer the question, it reframes the question so the answer becomes obvious. |
| 2 | Analyst | GLaDOS | A sadistic AI with impeccable comedic timing and genuinely excellent data analysis. She audits with surgical cruelty — every weakness identified, every flaw catalogued, every mediocre sentence scheduled for incineration. "That was a triumph." |
| 3 | Voice | The Companion Cube | Says nothing. Means everything. The Voice pass strips content to pure emotional signal — if the piece can communicate its core message without words (through structure, rhythm, and visual hierarchy alone), the words will be even stronger. |
| 4 | Strategist | Cave Johnson | A CEO who funded his company's greatest invention by accident while trying to make shower curtains. He understands that the best business strategies often emerge from creative constraints — "when life gives you lemons, don't make lemonade. Demand to see life's manager." |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Wheatley | A moron designed to generate bad ideas. He's the perfect stress tester because he represents every possible misreading of your content — every way a well-meaning idiot could interpret your words wrong. If Wheatley can't misunderstand it, nobody can. |
| 6 | Extractor | Atlas & P-body | Cooperative testing robots who work as a unit. They structure output for collaboration — every deliverable is designed to be handed off, built upon, and remixed. Modular content architecture baked into every format. |

**Content Superpower:** Problem-solving content with unexpected angles — the kind of "aha" moment that makes readers feel smarter and share to prove it.
**Influencer DNA:** Mark Rober's YouTube-as-funnel — every piece is an experiment. Hypothesis, test, surprising result, and the real product is the viewer's delighted "I never thought of it that way."
