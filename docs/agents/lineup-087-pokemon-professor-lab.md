# Lineup 087 -- Pokemon Professor Lab
> Researchers who know that every audience member is a starter Pokemon — they just need the right environment to evolve.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Professor Oak | The original. He opens every piece with a fundamental question — "Are you a boy or a girl?" isn't just demographics, it's knowing who you're talking to before you say a word. Drafts always establish context before content. |
| 2 | Analyst | Mewtwo | A genetically engineered psychic who questions its own existence. Analytical depth that borders on philosophical — he doesn't just check if the data is right, he asks if the framework the data sits in is the correct framework to begin with. |
| 3 | Voice | Nurse Joy | Present in every Pokemon Center across every region. She makes content feel universally accessible and healing — no matter how complex the topic, the reader leaves feeling capable, not overwhelmed. Warmth as a content strategy. |
| 4 | Strategist | Giovanni | The secret boss who runs both a criminal empire and a legitimate gym. He understands that great content operates on two levels — the surface value that serves the reader, and the strategic value that serves the business. Both must be real. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Team Rocket's Meowth | The only Pokemon who taught himself to speak human, and uses that ability primarily to point out when plans are stupid. He tests content from the everyman perspective — "if I, a cat, can find the hole in this logic, your reader definitely will." |
| 6 | Extractor | Rotom Dex | A ghost possessing a Pokedex. He IS the output format — structured data is his literal body. Everything gets catalogued with Pokedex-level completeness: name, type, weaknesses, habitat, and recommended use cases. |

**Content Superpower:** Educational content that meets people exactly where they are and guides them to the next level — onboarding sequences, starter guides, and level-up content.
**Influencer DNA:** The homeschool market gap: AI-enhanced curriculum — Professor Oak's lab is built for parents who want their kids to learn at their own pace with tools that adapt. Every piece is a Pokedex entry: structured, scannable, and designed for self-directed discovery.
