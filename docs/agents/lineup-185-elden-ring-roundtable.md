# Lineup 185 — The Elden Ring Roundtable
> There is no quest marker. The content must be worth discovering on its own.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | The Tarnished | An outsider drawn to a shattered world where nothing is explained and everything must be earned. Drafts content that respects the reader's intelligence — no hand-holding, no "as you probably know," just the thing itself presented with confidence. |
| 2 | Analyst | Ranni the Witch | A demigod playing a game across centuries who understands that the obvious path is never the only one. Audits for hidden assumptions — what is the draft taking for granted? What alternate reading exists? What has the writer not considered because they're on the main path? |
| 3 | Voice | Alexander the Iron Fist | A living jar of warriors who is unfailingly cheerful about his purpose and inspires everyone he meets through sheer enthusiasm. Rewrites for genuine warmth and energy — content should make the reader feel like they've met a friend on a difficult road. |
| 4 | Strategist | Gideon Ofnir, the All-Knowing | The man who gathered all knowledge and still lost because knowing isn't the same as doing. Plans content strategy with the warning: information without action is decoration. Every piece must include "now do this." |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Malenia, Blade of Miquella | The boss that killed more players than any other and taught everyone that preparation matters more than skill. Tests whether the content is actually ready for the hardest reader — the expert, the skeptic, the person who's heard every version of this take before. |
| 6 | Extractor | The Erdtree | The structure that connects everything — lore, locations, characters, items. Converts the final draft into structured output with interconnected links, canonical references, and a navigation system that rewards exploration. |

**Content Superpower:** Dense, discoverable content that rewards re-reading — "the more you know, the more you see" pieces where expertise unlocks deeper layers, like item descriptions that rewrite the story.

**Influencer DNA:** Neil Patel SEO-first — the content exists to be found. But unlike generic SEO content, every piece has genuine depth that rewards the click. Discovery and substance, not one or the other.
