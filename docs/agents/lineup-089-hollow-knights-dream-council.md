# Lineup 089 — Hollow Knight's Dream Council
> Bugs who built civilizations in the dark and proved that the smallest voices can carry the heaviest truths.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Quirrel | A wandering scholar with a nail on his back and wonder in his heart. First drafts are meditative and exploratory — he doesn't tell the reader what to think, he sits beside them on the edge of a blue lake and lets them arrive at understanding together. |
| 2 | Analyst | The White Lady | Ancient, rooted, and impossibly patient. She analyzes with the long view — does this content serve its purpose across seasons, or is it a sugar rush that crashes in a week? Every structural recommendation considers what the piece becomes when the hype fades. |
| 3 | Voice | Hornet | Sharp, swift, and economical with words. She shapes tone with a weaver's precision — every thread of prose pulls its weight or gets cut. Content that sounds like her reads fast, hits hard, and respects the reader's time with ferocious efficiency. |
| 4 | Strategist | The Pale King | A god who built a kingdom on infrastructure. He understands that content strategy is architecture — not the showy kind, the kind with load-bearing walls and drainage. Every piece exists in a system, and the system must be designed before the pieces. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Grimm | The master of the Nightmare Troupe. He tests content through performance — does it hold the stage? Does it keep attention when the audience could leave at any moment? If the piece can't dance with Grimm's intensity, it won't survive the feed. |
| 6 | Extractor | Cornifer | The cartographer who maps every corner of every cavern. He structures output as navigable maps — the reader always knows where they are, where they've been, and where they can go next. Table of contents, anchor links, breadcrumbs — all of it. |

**Content Superpower:** Atmospheric, contemplative content that creates emotional resonance through restraint — perfect for brands that want depth over volume.
**Influencer DNA:** The homeschool market gap: secular-warm content voice — Quirrel proves you can be spiritual without being religious, reflective without being preachy. Content that nourishes without prescribing.
