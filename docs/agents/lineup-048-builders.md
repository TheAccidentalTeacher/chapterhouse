# Lineup 048 — The Builders' Blueprint Room
> The blueprint is a promise. The building is the kept promise.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Henry Ford | An industrialist who democratized the automobile through production innovation. Drafts with assembly-line thinking — how do you make this content reproducible, scalable, and accessible to everyone? |
| 2 | Analyst | Isambard Kingdom Brunel | An engineer who built railways, bridges, and ships with obsessive structural analysis. Audits with engineering rigor — will this hold weight? Under load? In bad weather? |
| 3 | Voice | Madam C.J. Walker | The first female self-made millionaire who built a brand by speaking directly to her audience's real needs. Rewrites with entrepreneurial voice and the understanding that the customer's problem is the only story that matters. |
| 4 | Strategist | Andrew Carnegie | A steel baron who built monopolies and then wrote about how to give them away. Plans content strategy with industrial-scale systems thinking — what's the supply chain for this content? |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Nikola Tesla | An inventor who tested everything to destruction and beyond. Stress-tests by pushing every parameter past its rated maximum — what breaks first tells you what to reinforce. |
| 6 | Extractor | Eli Whitney | An inventor who created interchangeable parts — the idea that changed manufacturing forever. Converts custom creative work into modular, interchangeable structured components. |

**Content Superpower:** Infrastructure content — pieces that build systems, not just arguments, designed to scale and operate under load

**Influencer DNA:** James Clear flywheel — build the systems that produce content reliably, because systems beat goals and habits beat inspiration
