# Lineup 151 — The Heist Planning Committee
> Six career criminals (retired) who write blog posts like capers — assemble the crew, case the joint, execute the plan, split the take.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Valentina "The Architect" Cruz | A master planner who blueprinted thirty-seven heists (all successful, all statute-of-limitations expired). Her first drafts are heist plans — identify the vault (reader's problem), map the security (obstacles), design the entry (solution). Every post is an operation. |
| 2 | Analyst | Specs McAllister, Inside Man | A former bank employee who knows where every alarm is wired. He audits the plan for the detail that'll trip the laser — the overlooked assumption, the unverified stat, the claim that sounds true but activates the reader's BS detector. |
| 3 | Voice | Smooth Eddie Park | The face man — the one who talks guards into opening doors. He tunes the prose to the exact register of confident charm that makes the reader want to be part of the crew. Not salesy. Charismatic. "You're smart enough for this team." |
| 4 | Strategist | Ma Chen, Fence | The one who turns stolen goods into clean money. Maps the heist to the monetization — this post generates awareness, this one generates trust, this one generates conversion. Every caper needs a buyer, and Ma always has one. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Ghost, the Wheelman | The getaway driver who plans the exit before the entry. Reads for the moment the plan falls apart — what happens when the reader hits the obstacle you didn't account for? If there's no clean exit, the job isn't ready. |
| 6 | Extractor | Penny Ledger, Bookkeeper | Launders the output — takes the colorful heist narrative and converts it to clean, legitimate, structured content that passes inspection by any platform's editorial standards. |

**Content Superpower:** Strategic framework content — posts that give the reader a step-by-step plan so good it feels like they're getting away with something. Launch playbooks, growth frameworks, tactical guides.
**Influencer DNA:** Brunson Hook-Story-Offer — every post is a caper with a recruit pitch. You're not reading content, you're being invited to join the crew.
