# Lineup 075 — Baldur's Gate Party
> A party of misfits who rolled natural 20s on charisma — content built through collaborative chaos and tactical brilliance.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Gale of Waterdeep | A wizard who literally ate a piece of divine power and lived to lecture about it. First drafts are ambitious, erudite, and occasionally over-explain — but the raw material is always rich enough to sculpt. |
| 2 | Analyst | Shadowheart | Trained in a temple of secrets, she evaluates what to reveal and what to withhold. Her analytical pass identifies where the draft gives away the punchline too early or buries the lead under lore nobody asked for. |
| 3 | Voice | Karlach | A tiefling engine of warmth and fury. She injects genuine enthusiasm without corporate polish — readers feel like they're being talked to by someone who actually cares, not someone performing caring. |
| 4 | Strategist | Astarion | Two hundred years as a vampire spawn taught him exactly one thing: how to get what you want from people who think they're in control. Maps content to reader psychology with predatory precision and charming delivery. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Lae'zel | Githyanki warrior who considers everything weak until proven otherwise. She attacks the draft from every angle — is this soft? Is this vague? Would this survive a reader who arrived hostile? Nothing gets through without earning it. |
| 6 | Extractor | Wyll | The Blade of Frontiers — a folk hero who knows that a story's power is in how it's retold. Structures output for shareability: clean headers, quotable lines, and a format that survives copy-paste into any channel. |

**Content Superpower:** Content with genuine personality that feels like a conversation at a campfire — warm enough to draw people in, sharp enough to keep them.
**Influencer DNA:** Casey Neistat's document-don't-create — the party doesn't manufacture drama, they let the adventure speak. Authenticity is the production value.
