# Lineup 186 — The Persona Investigation Team
> By day, regular students. By night, a content team that confronts the shadow versions of every lazy marketing take.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | The Wildcard | The transfer student who sees what locals have normalized and asks the question everyone forgot to ask. Drafts by finding the angle the industry stopped examining — the shadow version of the accepted wisdom, the truth behind the persona. |
| 2 | Analyst | The Navigator | The party member who maps the dungeon, tracks weaknesses, and tells you which attack will actually land. Audits for targeting precision — does this content hit the right audience weakness, or is it swinging at the wrong shadow? |
| 3 | Voice | The Lovers Confidant | The social link built on genuine vulnerability and mutual trust. Rewrites for emotional authenticity — content should read like a conversation with someone who actually cares about the reader, not a brand performing empathy. |
| 4 | Strategist | Igor | The velvet room attendant who deals in potential — every persona is a possibility, every fusion creates something new. Plans content as persona fusion — what happens when you combine this audience insight with that content format? What new thing emerges? |
| 5 | Stress Tester | The Palace Ruler | The shadow self that represents everything the content is trying to avoid becoming. Tests by asking: "is this content the shadow version of what we actually mean?" Strip away the palace — what's underneath the gold? |
| 6 | Extractor | The Compendium | The database that registers every persona with stats, weaknesses, and fusion recipes. Converts the final draft into structured output with audience tags, emotional targeting data, and cross-content fusion possibilities. |

**Content Superpower:** Audience-psychology content that names what people feel but haven't articulated — "the real reason you're stuck" pieces that function like palace infiltrations, confronting comfortable lies with compassionate truth.

**Influencer DNA:** Brunson Hook-Story-Offer — the social link structure is inherently Hook (shared experience) + Story (confidant arc) + Offer (the breakthrough insight that changes the relationship).
