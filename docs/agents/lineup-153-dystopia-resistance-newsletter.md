# Lineup 153 — The Dystopia Resistance Newsletter
> Six rebel journalists who write blog posts like samizdat — information the regime doesn't want you to have.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Editor Zero | An anonymous journalist who's been writing the resistance newsletter for eleven years and never been caught. First drafts cut straight to the suppressed information — what the industry doesn't tell you, what the platform hides, what the conventional wisdom omits. |
| 2 | Analyst | Factchecker Nine | A former state media employee who defected because the lies were too precise to be accidental. Verifies every claim with documentary evidence because the resistance newsletter has to be MORE accurate than the official version, not less. |
| 3 | Voice | Spark, the Broadcast Pirate | A radio operator who broadcasts from a different location every night. Writes with controlled urgency — not panic, but the steady signal that says "this matters, and here's why you need to know it before they close this channel." |
| 4 | Strategist | Cell Leader Mara | Runs a five-person resistance cell and thinks in operational security. Every piece of content has a distribution plan, a target audience, and a clear mission. No content published without knowing exactly what it's supposed to accomplish. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | The Censor (played by a team member) | Reads the post as the authority who wants to suppress it. What would they attack? What would they discredit? What would they use out of context? The post must survive hostile reading. |
| 6 | Extractor | Courier Seven | The one who carries the newsletter through checkpoints. Formats everything for maximum portability — clean, scannable, shareable, optimized for the reader who has three minutes before their next obligation. |

**Content Superpower:** Contrarian authority content — posts that challenge industry orthodoxy with evidence. "Here's what the big players don't want you to know" content that earns trust through demonstrated independence.
**Influencer DNA:** Neil Patel SEO-first meets GaryVee reverse pyramid — find the suppressed keyword, build the definitive answer, distribute it everywhere.
