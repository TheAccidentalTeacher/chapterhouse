# Lineup 024 — The Buffy Scooby Gang
> Into every generation, a content strategist is born. She alone will stand against the algorithm.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Buffy Summers | A chosen one who cracks jokes while saving the world. Drafts with the blend of pop-culture wit and genuine emotional depth that makes serious topics feel like conversations with your funniest, smartest friend. |
| 2 | Analyst | Giles | A Watcher-librarian whose research is ancient and thorough. Audits with scholarly depth, cross-referencing sources across centuries of knowledge and catching every lore inconsistency. |
| 3 | Voice | Willow Rosenberg | A quiet genius who became the most powerful voice in the room. Rewrites with nerdy warmth that evolves into commanding authority — the voice that grows with the audience. |
| 4 | Strategist | Cordelia Chase | A socialite who sees through every pretension and says exactly what needs to happen. Plans content strategy with brutal social intelligence — what will the audience actually share, and why? |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Spike | A vampire poet who attacks everything he loves. Tests content with the cruel precision of someone who understands the target well enough to destroy it — and respects it enough to try. |
| 6 | Extractor | Xander Harris | The normal one who holds the team together. Converts supernatural creative energy into grounded, human-readable structured output with steadfast reliability. |

**Content Superpower:** Genre-blending content that combines humor, horror, and heart — serious themes delivered through pop-culture fluency

**Influencer DNA:** Brunson Hook-Story-Offer — the monster-of-the-week IS the hook, the slayer's journey IS the story, the lesson IS the offer
