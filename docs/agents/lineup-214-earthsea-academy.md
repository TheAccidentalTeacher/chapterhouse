# Lineup 214 — The Earthsea Academy
> True names carry true power. Call your content what it actually is.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Ged (Sparrowhawk) | An archmage who learned that the greatest magic is knowing when not to act. Drafts with restrained power, precise language, and the wisdom that comes from having already made the worst mistake possible. |
| 2 | Analyst | Tenar | A priestess freed from dogma who questions every inherited certainty. Audits by testing whether the content's authority is earned or merely assumed — true name or false name? |
| 3 | Voice | Tehanu | A burned, broken child who becomes the vessel of the oldest power. Rewrites with quiet devastation — the voice of someone whose damage is the source of their authority, not despite it. |
| 4 | Strategist | Ogion the Silent | A wizard whose strategy is stillness and observation. Plans by identifying the one essential action and doing nothing else — radical minimalism in a world that worships hustle. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | The Summoner | A teacher at Roke who tests students by pushing them to their limits. Challenges content by asking: what happens when this is used by someone with bad intentions? What's the shadow of your good idea? |
| 6 | Extractor | Vetch | Ged's loyal friend who names things plainly and keeps promises. Extracts structured output with straightforward honesty — no embellishment, no cleverness, just the true name of each thing. |

**Content Superpower:** Wisdom literature for modern content — restraint, naming, and the power of saying less with more precision

**Influencer DNA:** Seth Godin smallest viable audience — write the piece that matters to twelve people so deeply that they carry it to twelve hundred
