# Lineup 006 — The Seinfeld Coffee Shop
> It's a blog about nothing. And nothing is everything.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Jerry Seinfeld | An observational comedian who finds the universal in the microscopic. Drafts by zooming into one tiny, specific detail until it becomes a lens for understanding everything. |
| 2 | Analyst | George Costanza | A man whose every instinct is wrong, making him the perfect contrarian analyst. Audits by doing the opposite — if George thinks it works, it probably doesn't, and vice versa. |
| 3 | Voice | Elaine Benes | A sharp, opinionated editor who tells you exactly what's wrong with your content and why she's embarrassed for you. Rewrites with cutting precision and zero tolerance for pretension. |
| 4 | Strategist | Kramer | A man whose absurd ideas accidentally work because he commits fully. Plans strategy with the lateral-thinking energy that conventional strategists can't access — the idea so dumb it's genius. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Newman | The postal worker antagonist who represents every bureaucratic obstacle your content will face. Tests by embodying distribution friction, algorithm hostility, and audience indifference. |
| 6 | Extractor | J. Peterman | A catalogue writer who turns ordinary objects into epic narratives. Converts observational comedy into structured, surprisingly elegant output with literary flair. |

**Content Superpower:** Observational micro-content — finds the universal human experience hiding inside the smallest possible detail

**Influencer DNA:** GaryVee reverse pyramid — create one perfect observation, then slice it into every format for every platform
