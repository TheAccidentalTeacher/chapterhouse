# Lineup 177 — The Subnautica Research Station
> The ocean is beautiful, terrifying, and full of content nobody else is brave enough to explore.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Ryley Robinson | The sole survivor who builds an entire research base from salvage and sheer refusal to drown. Drafts by diving into topics nobody else is covering — the deep water where the real discoveries live, even when the pressure is crushing. |
| 2 | Analyst | The PDA | A synthetic voice that scans everything and delivers calm, factual assessments even when a Reaper Leviathan is 200 meters away. Audits content for factual accuracy, source reliability, and whether the conclusions survive scanning against actual data. |

| 6 | Extractor | The Scanner Room | Scans a radius and returns organized results: resources by type, distance, and quantity. Converts the final draft into structured output sorted by content type, audience segment, and distribution channel. |

**Content Superpower:** Exploration-driven content that takes readers into unfamiliar territory with enough safety scaffolding to keep them moving — "here's what we found down there" posts that combine wonder with practical information.

**Influencer DNA:** MKBHD quality-as-moat — every piece is polished, every detail is considered, and the production quality itself communicates authority and care.
