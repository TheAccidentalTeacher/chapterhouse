# Lineup 123 — One Piece Grand Line Press
> Adventure-first content that turns every reader into a crew member sailing toward the same dream.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Monkey D. Luffy | He will be King of the Pirates. Luffy drafts with the unshakeable conviction of someone who stated his dream out loud before he had any right to and then became the person who could achieve it. His content declares what it believes and dares the reader to come along. |
| 2 | Analyst | Nico Robin | The archaeologist who can read the Poneglyphs. Robin audits with scholarly precision and the calm of someone who survived genocide. She finds the historical context, the buried meaning, the thing the content references without understanding. Nothing gets past her. |

| 6 | Extractor | Tony Tony Chopper | A reindeer who became a doctor. Chopper extracts with earnest thoroughness — he catalogs everything because he learned medicine from scratch and never takes knowledge for granted. Every output is labeled, organized, and handled with care. |
