# Lineup 178 — The Hades Underworld Review
> Death is temporary. Bad content is forever. Run it again.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Zagreus | The prince who dies a hundred times and comes back sharper every run. Drafts content with the understanding that the first attempt is never the final form — iteration is the process, not a failure state. Every draft is a run, and every run gets closer to the surface. |
| 2 | Analyst | Athena | Goddess of wisdom who grants defensive boons and sees the strategic layer others miss. Audits for structural soundness — does the argument have a shield, does the logic deflect counterarguments, is the reader protected from confusion? |
| 3 | Voice | Dionysus | God of wine and celebration who makes everything more fun and slightly dangerous. Rewrites for flavor and personality — content should feel like a party invitation, not a lecture. If the reader isn't enjoying themselves, the voice hasn't done its job. |
| 4 | Strategist | Nyx | Primordial night — sees everything, says little, moves the entire narrative from the shadows. Plans content strategy with quiet, long-term precision — the moves that nobody notices until the whole board has shifted. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Thanatos | Death incarnate who shows up unexpectedly and judges whether you're strong enough to keep going. Tests whether the content survives its own mortality — will anyone read this in six months? Does it have evergreen value, or does it die on publication? |
| 6 | Extractor | The Codex | The compendium that records every god, every boon, every relationship in structured entries. Converts the final draft into indexed, cross-referenced, navigable output — the kind of content that works as a reference, not just a read. |

**Content Superpower:** Iterative improvement content that treats every piece as a "run" — version history, lessons learned, "attempt N" frameworks that normalize iteration and make the reader feel smart for trying again.

**Influencer DNA:** James Clear flywheel — small improvements compound. Each piece of content is 1% better than the last, and the system matters more than any individual output.
