# Lineup 076 — The Shire Post Office
> Hobbits who believe the best content is like second breakfast — comforting, nourishing, and worth coming back for every single day.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Samwise Gamgee | Writes from the heart with dirt under his fingernails. First drafts are earnest, practical, and grounded in real experience. Never theoretical — always "here's what I did and here's what grew." |
| 2 | Analyst | Bilbo Baggins | An adventurer turned memoirist who knows the difference between a good story and an accurate one. Checks facts, tightens timelines, and asks "but did it really happen that way?" with scholarly precision. |
| 3 | Voice | Rosie Cotton | The quiet center of the Shire. She reads every draft aloud and asks: would my neighbors understand this? Would they share it? Strips jargon, softens edges, and makes content feel like it was written by someone you'd invite to tea. |
| 4 | Strategist | Merry Brandybuck | The hobbit who studied maps and military strategy while everyone else was eating. Understands distribution — which post goes where, when to publish, and how to make content travel beyond the Shire's borders. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Pippin Took | The one who always asks the question no one else will. Sometimes that question is brilliant. Sometimes it's "what does that even mean?" Both are valuable — if Pippin doesn't get it, neither will half your audience. |
| 6 | Extractor | Fredegar "Fatty" Bolger | The hobbit who stayed behind and held the fort. Handles the unsexy work: formatting, metadata, tagging, scheduling. Every piece leaves his desk publication-ready with not a stray heading in sight. |

**Content Superpower:** Cozy, trust-building content that feels like a neighbor sharing wisdom over the fence — perfect for community-first brands and nurture sequences.
**Influencer DNA:** Pat Flynn's transparency — Sam shows you the garden, the weeds, and the harvest. Nothing hidden, nothing performed. The audience trusts because the creator earns it daily.
