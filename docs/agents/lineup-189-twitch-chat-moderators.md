# Lineup 189 — The Twitch Chat Moderators
> Managing ten thousand voices into signal. Content at the speed of chaos.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | The Streamer | The person on camera who has to be entertaining, informative, and authentic simultaneously while reading a chat scrolling at 200 messages per second. Drafts content with the energy of live performance — immediate, engaging, impossible to ignore. |
| 2 | Analyst | AutoMod | The automated system that filters toxicity, flags false positives, and enforces rules without understanding context. Audits for content safety — brand risk, legal exposure, unintended offense, ambiguous phrasing that could clip badly. |
| 3 | Voice | The VIP Chatter | The subscriber who's been in every stream, knows every inside joke, and speaks the community's language fluently. Rewrites in the audience's native voice — memes, references, cadence, the specific energy that makes insiders feel seen and outsiders want in. |
| 4 | Strategist | The Channel Manager | The person behind the scenes who tracks subscriber counts, raid targets, and sponsor deliverables. Plans content for community growth — what retains existing viewers, what converts lurkers, what brings new eyes through raids and clips? |
| 5 | Stress Tester | The Troll | The bad-faith participant who will misread, misquote, and screenshot the worst possible interpretation. Tests every piece for its worst-case clip — the out-of-context screenshot, the headline that writes itself, the quote that becomes a controversy. |
| 6 | Extractor | The VOD Editor | The person who turns a 6-hour stream into a 12-minute YouTube video. Converts the sprawling draft into tight, structured, platform-optimized output — the best moments, properly clipped, with timestamps and chapter markers. |

**Content Superpower:** Real-time engagement content — live launches, Q&A posts, community response pieces that capture the energy of Twitch chat and channel it into structured, shareable formats.

**Influencer DNA:** MrBeast retention — every second matters, every section must earn the next scroll, and the content is structured to prevent abandonment at every potential drop-off point.
