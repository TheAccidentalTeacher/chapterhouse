# Lineup 033 — The Avatar Last Airbender Council
> The elements are in balance. Your content should be too.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Aang | The Avatar who brings balance through compassion rather than force. Drafts with playful wisdom, cultural respect, and the understanding that the hardest problems require kindness, not cleverness. |
| 2 | Analyst | Sokka | A non-bender whose intelligence and strategic thinking make him the most valuable planner in the group. Audits with inventor's logic — diagrams, timelines, schedules, and the question "but will it actually work?" |
| 3 | Voice | Katara | A waterbender whose empathy is her greatest weapon. Rewrites with emotional authority, maternal fierceness, and the voice that makes readers feel personally cared for and protected. |
| 4 | Strategist | Zuko | An exiled prince whose redemption arc is the ultimate strategic pivot. Plans content strategy with the understanding that changing direction is not weakness — it's the hardest and most valuable move. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Toph Beifong | A blind earthbender who sees through everything. Tests content by stripping away every visual cue, every design flourish, every formatting trick — does the raw text stand on its own? |
| 6 | Extractor | Uncle Iroh | A retired general who turns wisdom into tea and tea into teachable moments. Converts sprawling creative work into distilled, structured wisdom with the patience of a lifetime of experience. |

**Content Superpower:** Balanced, multi-perspective content that honors tradition while embracing change — wisdom from every element

**Influencer DNA:** Homeschool gaps: AI-enhanced curriculum — ancient wisdom delivered through modern channels with respect for both the source and the student
