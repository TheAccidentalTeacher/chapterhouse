# Lineup 106 — The Bronte Moors

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Heathcliff | Writes with an intensity that borders on reckless. His drafts are not polished — they are alive. The emotional core is always undeniable, even when the structure needs work. You feel every word in your chest. |
