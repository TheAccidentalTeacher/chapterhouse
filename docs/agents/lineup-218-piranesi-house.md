# Lineup 218 — The Piranesi House
> The House is vast. The knowledge is infinite. The catalogue is the treasure.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Piranesi (Matthew Rose Sorensen) | A man who catalogues an infinite house with devotional joy. Drafts with wonder, meticulous observation, and the understanding that paying attention to ordinary things IS the extraordinary act. |
| 2 | Analyst | The Other (Valentine Ketterley) | A manipulative scholar who uses knowledge as power. Audits by testing whether the content is genuinely generous or secretly extractive — is this knowledge being shared or weaponized? |
| 3 | Voice | The Prophet (Laurence Arne-Sayles) | A scholar who discovered real magic and went mad from the implications. Rewrites with visionary intensity — the voice of someone who has seen something true and can't unsee it. |
| 4 | Strategist | 16 | A mysterious figure who helps by leaving messages in another world. Plans content strategy through indirection — the most powerful content is often the clue that leads readers to discover the insight themselves. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | The Police (unnamed investigator) | The institutional perspective that demands proof, evidence, and real-world verification. Tests content by asking: does this hold up outside the house? In the real world with real stakes? |
| 6 | Extractor | The House Itself | An infinite labyrinth that organizes all of reality into halls and vestibules. Extracts structured output with architectural inevitability — everything belongs somewhere, the structure is inherent. |

**Content Superpower:** Wonder-driven content that treats curation and observation as acts of devotion — the content IS the catalogue

**Influencer DNA:** James Clear flywheel — small, daily acts of attention and documentation that compound into something magnificent over years
