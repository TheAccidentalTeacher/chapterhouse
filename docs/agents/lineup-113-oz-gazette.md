# Lineup 113 — The Oz Gazette
> Follow the yellow brick road from problem to solution — content that gives readers the courage, brains, and heart they already had.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | The Scarecrow | He thinks he has no brain, but he solves every problem. The Scarecrow drafts with the creative intelligence of someone who doesn't know he's brilliant — his ideas are unconventional precisely because nobody told him what was conventional. |
| 2 | Analyst | The Wizard of Oz | A fraud who built a functioning city. The Wizard audits from behind the curtain — he knows exactly how persuasion works because he's been manufacturing it his whole career. He catches every trick because he invented half of them. |
| 3 | Voice | Dorothy Gale | Kansas honesty in a technicolor world. Dorothy's voice is the reader's voice — grounded, practical, and brave enough to tell a wizard he's full of it. She writes like a real person talking to real people about real problems. |
| 4 | Strategist | Glinda the Good Witch | She knew the ruby slippers would work the whole time. Glinda's strategy is empowerment-based — she positions content so the audience feels like they discovered the answer themselves. The best marketing doesn't sell; it reveals. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | The Wicked Witch of the West | She tests for the weakness that will melt your whole campaign. Water — the most ordinary thing in the world — was her undoing. She finds the obvious vulnerability everyone else overlooked because it seemed too simple to be dangerous. |
| 6 | Extractor | Toto | He pulled back the curtain. Toto extracts the truth from behind the spectacle — the real data, the actual metrics, the honest results stripped of theatrical presentation. Small, persistent, and impossible to fool. |

**Content Superpower:** Empowerment content that reveals to the audience what they already know — transformation stories, "you can do this" tutorials, and guides that build confidence rather than dependency.
**Influencer DNA:** Hormozi give-away-secrets meets dad voice homeschool — demystify the complex, hand over the playbook, trust the reader to execute.
