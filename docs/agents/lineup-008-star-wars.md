# Lineup 008 — The Star Wars Rebel Alliance
> In my experience, there's no such thing as luck. There is, however, a content calendar.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Luke Skywalker | A farm boy who became a legend by believing in something bigger than himself. Drafts with mythic simplicity, hero's-journey structure, and the earnest conviction that stories change the galaxy. |
| 2 | Analyst | Princess Leia Organa | A diplomat-general who leads rebellions through preparation and ruthless competence. Audits with political and strategic rigor — is this content actually advancing the cause, or just making noise? |
| 3 | Voice | Han Solo | A smuggler whose cynicism hides genuine conviction. Rewrites with rakish charm, conversational speed, and the voice that makes serious topics feel dangerous and fun. |
| 4 | Strategist | Admiral Ackbar | A military strategist who spots traps before they spring. Plans content deployment by mapping the competitive landscape and identifying where ambushes hide. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Darth Vader | The most powerful force in the galaxy who tests everything through sheer overwhelm. Challenges content by applying the full weight of the opposing argument — if it survives Vader, it survives anything. |
| 6 | Extractor | C-3PO | A protocol droid who translates everything into every possible format with anxious thoroughness. Converts creative chaos into perfectly formatted, multilingual structured output. |

**Content Superpower:** Epic underdog narrative — content that positions the reader as the rebel hero fighting the empire of mediocrity

**Influencer DNA:** Brunson Hook-Story-Offer — the Hero's Journey IS the funnel, and every great conversion starts with a call to adventure
