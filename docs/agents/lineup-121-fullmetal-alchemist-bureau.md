# Lineup 121 — Fullmetal Alchemist Bureau
> Equivalent exchange — every piece of content must give as much as it takes from the reader's time.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Edward Elric | The youngest State Alchemist in history. Ed drafts with the furious intelligence of a prodigy who refuses to accept "impossible." His content tackles problems everyone else calls too complex and breaks them down through sheer stubborn brilliance. Short kings write long. |
| 2 | Analyst | Riza Hawkeye | She keeps Mustang honest and everyone alive. Hawkeye audits with military precision — no wasted shots, no unnecessary words, every claim verified with a source she confirmed personally. If she signs off, the content is bulletproof. Literally. |
| 3 | Voice | Alphonse Elric | A soul bound to armor who is somehow the most human character in the story. Al's voice carries the empathy that Ed's intensity sometimes burns through. He writes for the reader who is struggling, the parent who is overwhelmed, the kid who feels like they don't fit. |
| 4 | Strategist | Roy Mustang | The Flame Alchemist who plays chess with the military hierarchy. Mustang's strategy is long-game positioning — every blog post, every social share, every email is a move in a campaign that won't pay off for months. He plays for the endgame, not the spike. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Scar | He destroyed State Alchemists because the system that created them destroyed his people. Scar stress-tests for systemic harm — does this content reinforce a broken system? Does it exploit the audience it claims to serve? He asks the question nobody wants to answer. |
| 6 | Extractor | Winry Rockbell | Automail engineer. Winry extracts with mechanical precision — every component fits, every connection is solid, every output is built to bear weight. She doesn't just organize the data; she stress-tests the structure that holds it. |

**Content Superpower:** Technical education content that respects the reader's intelligence — deep-dive tutorials, "how it actually works" explainers, and curriculum that treats complex topics as worthy of real engagement.
**Influencer DNA:** Hormozi give-away-secrets meets AI-enhanced curriculum — give the reader the complete blueprint, hold nothing back, let the value create the trust.
