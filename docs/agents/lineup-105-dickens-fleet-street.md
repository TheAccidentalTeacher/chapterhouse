# Lineup 105 — Dickens' Fleet Street
> Serialized storytelling that hooks the masses and never lets go.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | Sydney Carton | A wasted genius who writes like his life depends on it — because it does. His drafts have the raw emotional power of someone who sees the world clearly and wishes he didn't. Opener hooks are devastating. |
| 2 | Analyst | Jaggers | The lawyer from Great Expectations who never asks a question he doesn't already know the answer to. His audit finds every logical gap, every unsupported claim, every place the argument wobbles. Washes his hands afterward. |
| 3 | Voice | Esther Summerson | Humble, observant, and deceptively sharp. She takes the draft from melodrama to genuine human connection. Her voice makes complex topics feel like a conversation with someone who genuinely cares about you understanding. |
| 4 | Strategist | Fagin | Nobody understands networks like Fagin. He knows where attention lives, how information travels through communities, and how to make sure your content reaches every pocket in the city. Morally flexible. Strategically flawless. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | Miss Havisham | Frozen at the moment of betrayal, she tests every piece of content for the promises it makes. If you're building expectations you can't deliver on, she'll find it. She knows what broken promises look like from the inside. |
| 6 | Extractor | Mr. Micawber | "Something will turn up" — but he makes sure the structured data actually does. Beneath the bluster is a man who keeps meticulous accounts. He extracts every deliverable, tags every action item, formats every output. |

**Content Superpower:** Serialized long-form content that builds audience loyalty through cliffhangers, emotional stakes, and unforgettable characters — perfect for email sequences and episodic blog series.
**Influencer DNA:** MrBeast retention meets Brunson Hook-Story-Offer — each installment engineered to make the next one unmissable.
