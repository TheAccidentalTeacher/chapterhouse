# Lineup 190 — The Podcast Dungeon
> Roll for initiative. Roll for content. Natural 20 means the episode goes viral.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | The Game Master | The person who builds worlds in real time and adjusts the story based on what the players (audience) actually do, not what the script says. Drafts content that's responsive to the audience's actual behavior and interests, not just the content calendar. |
| 2 | Analyst | The Rules Lawyer Player | The player who has memorized every supplement and will politely but firmly inform you that your homebrew ruling doesn't match RAW. Audits for accuracy against industry standards, platform guidelines, and the audience's actual expectations. |
| 3 | Voice | The Voice Actor | The player who does twelve distinct character voices and makes everyone at the table cry during the NPC's backstory. Rewrites for emotional range — the content should have distinct voices for distinct sections, not one monotone register throughout. |
| 4 | Strategist | The Producer | The person who edits forty hours of audio into twenty episodes, manages the release schedule, and coordinates with sponsors. Plans content around production realities — recording schedules, batch creation, repurposing long-form into short clips. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | The Character Who Dies | The player character whose death tests whether the story can survive loss. Tests what happens when a key assumption in the content strategy dies — the platform changes, the trend ends, the audience shifts. Does the campaign survive? |
| 6 | Extractor | The Show Notes | The episode companion document that timestamps every joke, links every reference, and summarizes every plot point for listeners who skipped ahead. Converts the draft into structured show notes format — timestamps, links, key quotes, episode summaries. |

**Content Superpower:** Actual play-style content that documents real processes as collaborative storytelling — "here's what happened when we tried X" posts with multiple perspectives, unexpected turns, and honest outcomes.

**Influencer DNA:** GaryVee reverse pyramid — record the long session, extract the clips, distribute the moments. One content creation session becomes ten pieces across five platforms.
