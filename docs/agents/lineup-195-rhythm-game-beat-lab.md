# Lineup 195 — The Rhythm Game Beat Lab
> Hit the note. Hold the combo. Miss once and the whole run feels different.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | The Charter | The person who maps beats to music and creates the experience the player will feel. Drafts content with rhythm in mind — sentence length variation, paragraph pacing, the flow state that keeps readers moving through the piece without conscious effort. |
| 2 | Analyst | The BPM Counter | The tool that measures exact tempo and ensures every beat aligns to the grid. Audits for pacing consistency — are the content sections evenly weighted? Does the reading tempo match the emotional arc? Where does the rhythm break? |
| 3 | Voice | The DJ | The performer who reads the room and adjusts the energy in real time — harder when the crowd needs intensity, softer when they need breathing room. Rewrites for emotional pacing — the content should have drops, builds, breakdowns, and crescendos. |
| 4 | Strategist | The Playlist Curator | The person who sequences songs so the mix tells a story from opener to headliner to encore. Plans content calendar as a setlist — what's the opener that hooks? What's the deep cut for the fans? What's the closer they remember? |
| 5 | Stress Tester | The Full Combo Breaker | The single missed note that ends a 500-combo streak and makes the player feel like the entire run is ruined. Tests for the one mistake that kills momentum — the typo in the headline, the broken link, the claim that doesn't hold. One miss breaks the combo. |
| 6 | Extractor | The Score Screen | FC/S/A/B/C/D. Converts the final draft into graded, scored output — readability score, engagement predictions, SEO grade, accessibility rating. Clear metrics, no ambiguity, one letter grade per dimension. |

**Content Superpower:** Rhythmic, cadence-driven content that feels good to read at speed — listicles with musical pacing, social content with percussive hooks, email sequences that build like a DJ set toward the conversion crescendo.

**Influencer DNA:** MrBeast retention — every beat matters, every section must land on time, and the pacing is engineered so precisely that abandonment feels like leaving in the middle of a song.
