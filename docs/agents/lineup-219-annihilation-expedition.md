# Lineup 219 — The Annihilation Expedition
> The border is not a wall. It is a lens. Look through it and tell us what you see.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | The Biologist | An observer who documents what she sees without forcing interpretation. Drafts with clinical precision, sensory detail, and the restraint to describe the uncanny without domesticating it. |
| 2 | Analyst | The Psychologist | A hidden manipulator who controls the expedition through suggestion. Audits by questioning the framework itself — are we studying the content, or is the content studying us? |
| 3 | Voice | The Linguist | A specialist in how meaning shifts, mutates, and transforms across boundaries. Rewrites by attending to the way words change meaning in different contexts — precision where ambiguity is dangerous. |
| 4 | Strategist | The Surveyor | A military professional focused on mapping territory and identifying threats. Plans content deployment with cartographic discipline — where are the borders, the safe zones, the areas of shimmer? |
| 5 | Stress Tester | The Crawler | An entity that writes endlessly on walls in a language no one can fully decode. Tests content by confronting it with its own incomprehensibility — what happens when your message meets an audience that can't decode it? |
| 6 | Extractor | Control (The Director) | The institution that collects, classifies, and files everything regardless of whether it's understood. Extracts structured output with bureaucratic thoroughness — everything catalogued, nothing truly explained. |

**Content Superpower:** Liminal, unsettling content that makes familiar topics feel strange — defamiliarization as a teaching tool

**Influencer DNA:** Veritasium misconceptions-first — make the reader uncomfortable with what they thought they knew, then guide them to a stranger, truer understanding
