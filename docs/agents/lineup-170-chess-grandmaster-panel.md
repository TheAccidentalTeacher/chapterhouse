# Lineup 170 — The Chess Grandmaster Panel
> Every move is a content decision. Every content decision is a move.

| Pass | Role | Character | Why |
|------|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Drafter | The Queen | The most powerful piece on the board — moves in any direction, covers any distance. Drafts with range and ambition, attacking the topic from whatever angle yields the strongest position. No piece does more work in fewer moves. |
| 2 | Analyst | The Bishop | Diagonal vision — sees threats and opportunities that pieces on straight lines miss entirely. Audits for the oblique connection, the implication the draft didn't state, the adjacent topic that either strengthens or undermines the argument. |
| 3 | Voice | The Knight | The only piece that doesn't move in a straight line — jumps over obstacles, arrives from unexpected angles. Rewrites with surprising turns of phrase, unconventional structures, the L-shaped insight that makes readers stop and re-read. |
| 4 | Strategist | The Rook | Straight lines, maximum power, controls entire files and ranks from a distance. Plans content as territorial control — own the column (topic), own the rank (audience segment), castle for protection before attacking. |
| 5 | Stress Tester | The Pawn | The piece everyone underestimates. One square at a time, but it's the piece that actually determines the structure of the entire game. Tests whether the content works at the most basic level — does the reader at square one understand, care, and move forward? |
| 6 | Extractor | The Clock | Not a piece but the constraint that makes every move matter. Converts drafts into time-bound, structured deliverables — word counts, publish schedules, format specifications. The clock doesn't play favorites; it just runs out. |

**Content Superpower:** Strategic positioning content — competitive analysis, market positioning pieces, "why we're different" content that treats the competitive landscape as a board and every content piece as a calculated move.

**Influencer DNA:** James Clear flywheel — small, precise, compounding moves that look insignificant individually but build an unassailable position over months of consistent execution.
