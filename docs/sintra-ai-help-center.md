# Introduction

## What is Sintra?

Sintra is a digital assistant platform that combines multiple AI "helpers," a central Brain AI knowledge base, and several automation workflows. It is designed to streamline business processes ranging from customer support and copywriting to data analysis, recruitment, and social media management.

Each helper is specialized for a particular domain (e.g., Cassie handles support queries, Dexter performs data analysis, Penn writes copy). Users interact with helpers through chat-like conversations and can customize the tone, length, and language of responses.

### Who is Sintra designed for?

Sintra is designed for people wanting to:

- Utilize AI to work more efficiently and save time without having technical knowledge
- Manage multiple businesses or projects in one place
- Manage their single business or project in a single place

When building Sintra, the team thinks of small business owners, agencies, and freelancers; however, it can be utilized by anyone working on their business, project, or simply wanting to automate their workflows.

### Key Features

**Brain AI** — A knowledge management module that stores memories, webpages, and media; it personalizes helper responses based on your business information and integrates with external accounts.

**AI Helpers** — 12 helpers, each with a unique persona and skill set. They can draft emails, write blog posts, analyze data, brainstorm product ideas, perform SEO audits, and more. Some helpers have automations that can run in the background, and all helpers have their own set of use cases that act as instant tasks.

**Automations** — Standalone workflows that operate outside of the chat environment. They run in the background after setup, requiring minimal input. Available automations include Social Media Manager, Inbox Manager, Meeting Notetaker, Daily Summarizer, and Facebook Commenter.

**Recurring Helper Tasks** — Scheduled tasks that run on a set timetable without repeating the same instruction every time. Managed from the Tasks tab in the sidebar.

**Inbox & History** — A unified Inbox for ideas generated by helpers, completed tasks awaiting review, and a Chat History panel for past conversations.

**Settings & Billing** — Personal details, language preferences, password and subscription management, and experimental features.

**Referral Program** — Share your unique code to give and get free access to Sintra for a month.

### FAQ

**Can I generate a sequence of images using the same theme?**
Yes – specify this in your request. AI does not automatically remember the previous image. Specify the details you liked about the previously generated image and clarify what you'd like to see in the new one.

**Are these Helpers the same as ChatGPT?**
Sintra Helpers offer more than ChatGPT. You can set up knowledge libraries in Brain AI and connect other platforms so the helpers can post to social media and send emails for you, all in one place.

## Sintra AI Workspaces Explained

### What is a Workspace?

A workspace in Sintra is your working environment for a single project, client, or business. The workspace is designed to gather and store information for one business. If you have multiple projects with separate information, social media, or email accounts, create separate workspaces for each one.

Each workspace has its own:
- **Inbox** — Shows tasks requiring your input
- **Brain AI profile** — Contains information on your business/project and allows integrations
- **Subscription plan**
- **Helpers** — Aware only of context stored in this workspace's Brain AI
- **Automations** — Work quietly in the background
- **Chat history** — Conversations with that workspace's Helpers

### Types of Workspaces

**Individual Workspaces** — Default type. Only you can access it with your email and password.

**Team Workspaces** — Upgrade any workspace to team for free. Allows sharing access to Brain AI and Helpers with team members. Team members have their own logins and don't need a Sintra subscription.

### Multiple Workspaces

- One workspace created by default when you sign up
- Create unlimited workspaces for free
- Main workspace comes with 250 credits; additional free workspaces include 20 credits
- Each workspace has its own knowledge base, integration connections, and Helper chats
- Helpers can only access information within their own workspace

### Adding a New Workspace

1. Click on the workspace icon at the top-left corner
2. Click the "+" sign
3. Give the workspace a name and choose a color

### Removing a Workspace

1. Go to the workspace you wish to remove
2. Go to the Brain AI tab
3. Click "Manage" button above Brain AI profile card
4. Click "Delete Profile" and confirm
5. Deleted profiles cannot be retrieved

### Switching Between Workspaces

1. Click on the workspace icon in the upper-left corner (the colored button)
2. Click on the workspace from the list
3. Verify by checking the Brain AI tab

## Brain AI Explained

Brain AI is a powerful feature in Sintra that allows you to create a personalized knowledge base for your Helpers. By adding information to Brain AI, you're building the "brains" of your AI Helpers.

### What You Can Do with Brain AI

- Create unlimited Brain AI profiles as separate workspaces
- Add, edit, search, and remove various types of information (text, links, files, images, videos)
- Set up integration connections to different platforms
- Enhance your Helpers' ability to create personalized, context-rich content

### How to Locate Brain AI

- **Web app:** Brain icon in the left-side navigation panel
- **Mobile app:** Bottom-right corner

### Brain AI Tab Contents

- Quick overview of the Workspace (switch types, edit name, add profile image, change theme color)
- List of available integrations
- All knowledge collected in the workspace ("All Knowledge" section)

### Adding Information to Brain AI

Four types of information:
1. **Textual information** — Choose whether to summarize or not
2. **Webpage links** — Paste a publicly accessible URL (must allow crawling)
3. **Files** — Up to 20MB in multiple formats:
   - Images: JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF, WEBP
   - Videos: MP4, AVI, WEBM, MOV
   - Spreadsheets: CSV, XLSX, XLS
   - Text documents: TXT, PDF, DOCX, ODT, MD, JSON, HTML, HTM, XML
4. **Connections to other platforms** — Available as integrations

**Pro Tip:** Quickly add information from chats by clicking the +🧠 button near a sent or received message.

### Use Cases

**For Businesses:** Store company information, guidelines, brand voice, products, and more.

**For Agencies and Freelancers:** Create profiles for different clients; manage multiple brand identities.

**For Individuals:** Create separate profiles for work, lifestyle, and personal activities.

## Understanding AI Hallucinations

AI hallucinations occur when AI generates something that sounds confident and coherent but is actually wrong, misleading, or made up.

**Why it happens:**
- Models predict the next word based on training data — sometimes incorrectly
- Incomplete or outdated training data
- Vague instructions lead to misinterpretation

**How to manage hallucinations:**
- Be super specific with prompts
- Always double-check AI output
- Try rephrasing if results aren't right
- Use Brain AI wisely for consistent information
- Understand specific features and limitations

# General Troubleshooting & FAQ

## How to Reach Customer Support

- **Live Chat:** Click the chat button in the lower right corner of help.sintra.ai or the blue "Help" button in the Sintra App
- **Email:** help@sintra.ai and help@trysintra.com
- AI assistant Cassie manages live chat interactions
- Support available 24/7

## Common Issues

### Account Not Activated
Check bank statement, wait up to 1 hour for processing, then contact support.

### Unable to Select Social Media Pages
Reconnect integration, ensure proper permissions are granted.

### Email Typo at Checkout
Contact support to update the email associated with your account.

### Unable to Post to Facebook/Instagram
Check integration connections, reconnect if necessary.

### Timezone Issues
Change timezone in account settings to affect when Helpers schedule tasks.

### Reconnecting an Integration
Disconnect the existing integration and set it up again from scratch.

### Error Messages

- **Authentication Error:** Clear browser cache, try incognito mode
- **Loading Chunk 3908 Failed:** Clear cache and hard refresh
- **Function Call Unexpected Keyword:** Open new chat and retry
- **"There was an issue" Error:** Disconnect and reconnect integration
- **"You need to be subscribed" Error:** Verify subscription is active
- **"Access denied" for Images:** Try different browser or clear cache
- **Network Error:** Check internet connection, try refreshing
- **AxiosError 400 at Checkout:** Coupon code expired or invalid

### Login Issues

- **Didn't receive OTP:** Check spam folder, wait a few minutes, try again
- **Blank login screen:** Clear browser cache, try different browser
- **Unable to press "continue with email":** Disable browser extensions, try incognito
- **Blank app after login (mobile):** Reinstall the app

### Helper Issues

- **Helpers don't help:** Provide more context, check Brain AI for correct info
- **Canvas keeps loading:** Refresh the page, try new chat
- **Helper stops responding:** Open new chat, try again
- **Cross-posting issues:** Reconnect both platforms
- **Helper says "will do later":** This means the task requires your approval; check inbox

### Why We Offer Certain Troubleshooting Steps

Common steps like opening a new chat or clearing cache help resolve technical issues by resetting state and clearing corrupted data.
